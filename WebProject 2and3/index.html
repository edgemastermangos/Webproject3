People Involved in Treaty 1

The people who were involved in Treaty 1 include Brokenhead Ojibway Nation, Long Plain First Nation, Peguis First Nation, Roseau River Anishinaabe First Nation, Sagkeeng First Nation, Sandy Bay Ojibway First Nation, and Swan Lake First Nation.

(source:https://treaty1.ca/?s=treaty+1 )

Broken Promises

One of the promises was that two-thirds of the land was going to be designated as "Indian Land". Another promise was that they would continue to hunt/fish/trap on their ancestral lands.

(source:https://treaty1.ca/?s=treaty+1 )

What Makes Treaty 1 Different

What makes Treaty 1 different from other treaties is the fact that it was the FIRST numbered treaty whilst all the others came after it, making it the OG.

(source:https://treaty1.ca/?s=treaty+1 )

What Is Currently Happening in Terms of Treaty 1

Currently they are making 45 hectares into a mixed-neighbourhood. Also, the land was officially repatriated to the First Nations in December 2022.
